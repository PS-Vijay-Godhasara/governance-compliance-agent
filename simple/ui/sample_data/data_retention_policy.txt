COMPANY DATA RETENTION POLICY

Effective Date: January 1, 2024
Version: 2.1

1. PURPOSE
This policy establishes guidelines for the retention and disposal of company data to ensure compliance with legal requirements and business needs.

2. SCOPE
This policy applies to all employees, contractors, and third parties handling company data.

3. DATA CATEGORIES

3.1 Customer Personal Data
- Retention Period: 7 years after account closure
- Includes: Names, addresses, phone numbers, email addresses
- Legal Basis: Financial regulations, tax requirements

3.2 Financial Records
- Retention Period: 10 years
- Includes: Transaction records, invoices, payment data
- Legal Basis: SOX compliance, tax regulations

3.3 Employee Records
- Retention Period: 7 years after employment termination
- Includes: Personnel files, payroll records, performance reviews
- Legal Basis: Employment law, tax requirements

3.4 Marketing Data
- Retention Period: 2 years or until consent withdrawn
- Includes: Email preferences, campaign responses
- Legal Basis: Legitimate interest, consent

4. DATA DISPOSAL
- Secure deletion methods required
- Certificate of destruction for physical media
- Audit trail maintained for all disposals

5. COMPLIANCE REQUIREMENTS
- GDPR Article 5(1)(e) - Storage limitation
- CCPA Section 1798.105 - Right to deletion
- SOX Section 802 - Document retention
- HIPAA 45 CFR 164.316 - Administrative safeguards

6. EXCEPTIONS
Data may be retained longer if:
- Legal hold requirements
- Ongoing litigation
- Regulatory investigation
- Business continuity needs

7. REVIEW
This policy is reviewed annually and updated as needed to maintain compliance with evolving regulations.
