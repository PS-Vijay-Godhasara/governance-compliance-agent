Customer Data Processing Policy

Our company processes customer data for the following purposes:
- Account creation and management
- Transaction processing and monitoring
- Customer support and communication
- Marketing and promotional activities
- Compliance with regulatory requirements

Data Collection:
We collect personal information including names, email addresses, phone numbers, addresses, and financial information. This data is collected directly from customers during registration and through their use of our services.

Data Storage:
Customer data is stored in secure databases with encryption at rest and in transit. Access is restricted to authorized personnel only.

Data Retention:
We retain customer data for 7 years after account closure to comply with financial regulations. Marketing data is retained for 2 years unless the customer opts out.

Third Party Sharing:
We may share data with:
- Payment processors for transaction handling
- Credit agencies for risk assessment
- Regulatory authorities when required by law
- Service providers under strict confidentiality agreements

Customer Rights:
Customers have the right to access, correct, delete, or port their personal data. They can also object to processing or withdraw consent at any time.

This policy complies with GDPR, CCPA, and other applicable data protection regulations.
